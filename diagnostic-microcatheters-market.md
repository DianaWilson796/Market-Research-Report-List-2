<p><p>The diagnostic microcatheters market is segmented into two main types: single lumen microcatheters and double lumen microcatheters. Single lumen microcatheters are designed for precise delivery of contrast agents or therapeutic materials through a single channel, allowing for enhanced navigation in complex vascular structures. Double lumen microcatheters feature two separate channels, enabling concurrent delivery of different substances or simultaneous imaging and therapy. Both types are crucial in minimally invasive procedures, providing improved access and maneuverability in diagnostic applications.</p></p>
