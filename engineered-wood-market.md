<p><p>The engineered wood market comprises various products, including plywood, blockboard, hardboard, MDF (medium-density fiberboard), and particle board. Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer, providing strength and versatility. Blockboard consists of a core of softwood strips, sandwiched between plywood layers, ideal for making sturdy furniture. Hardboard is created from compressed wood fibers, known for its durability. MDF, made from wood fibers bonded with resin, offers a smooth surface for finishing. Particle board is composed of wood particles, delivering cost-effective solutions for furniture and cabinetry. Each type serves unique applications in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
