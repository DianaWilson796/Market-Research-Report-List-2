<p><p>Volumetric infusion pumps are categorized into single-channel and multi-channel devices. Single-channel pumps are designed to deliver fluids or medications to one patient at a time, providing simplicity and ease of use, making them ideal for basic infusion needs. In contrast, multi-channel pumps can administer multiple infusions simultaneously to several patients or deliver different medications to the same patient, enhancing efficiency in clinical settings. Both types cater to varying healthcare requirements, ensuring precise fluid management.</p></p>
