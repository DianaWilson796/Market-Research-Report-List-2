<p><p>The machining fluid market is categorized into various types, including synthesis machining fluids and semi-synthetic machining fluids. Synthesis machining fluids are fully synthetic options that offer superior lubrication and cooling properties without mineral oil, making them suitable for high-performance applications. On the other hand, semi-synthetic machining fluids combine both synthetic and mineral oil components, providing a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. These fluids are designed to enhance machining efficiency while reducing friction, wear, and thermal buildup during metalworking processes.</p></p>
