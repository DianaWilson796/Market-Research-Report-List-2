<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Wedge Brakes</li><li>Double Chamber Wedge Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wedge brakes are a type of braking system used primarily in heavy vehicles and industrial applications. The single chamber wedge brake features one hydraulic chamber, allowing for efficient engagement and disengagement of the braking mechanism. In contrast, double chamber wedge brakes incorporate two chambers, providing enhanced braking power and stability. This design allows for more even force distribution, improving overall performance and safety. Both types serve to efficiently convert hydraulic pressure into mechanical force, ensuring reliable stopping capabilities.</p></p>
