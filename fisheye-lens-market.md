<p><p>The fisheye lens market is categorized into circular fisheye lenses and diagonal fisheye lenses. Circular fisheye lenses provide a 180-degree view, resulting in a perfectly circular image that captures vast scenes, ideal for artistic and panoramic photography. Diagonal fisheye lenses, on the other hand, offer a slightly wider field of view, typically around 180 degrees diagonally, creating a rectangular image that maintains a more standard aspect ratio suitable for various applications, including landscapes and creative photography.</p></p>
