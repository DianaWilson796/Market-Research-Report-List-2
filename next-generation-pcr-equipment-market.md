<p><p>The Next Generation PCR Equipment market is divided into two primary types: Two-Step Next Generation PCR and One-Step Next Generation PCR. Two-Step Next Generation PCR involves an initial reverse transcription process followed by PCR amplification, allowing for greater sensitivity and specificity in detecting RNA targets. In contrast, One-Step Next Generation PCR combines both reverse transcription and amplification in a single step, streamlining the process and reducing time and contamination risks. Both methods are integral in genomics and diagnostics.</p></p>
