<p><strong>The Liquid Waterborne Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexography Inks</li><li>Gravure Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid waterborne printing inks market comprises mainly two types: flexography inks and gravure inks. Flexography inks are designed for high-speed printing on various substrates, offering quick drying times and excellent adhesion. They are commonly used in packaging and labels. Gravure inks, on the other hand, are used for high-quality, continuous printing on flexible materials and are ideal for long runs due to their consistency and vibrant color reproduction. Both types prioritize eco-friendliness and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
