<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Laboratory Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Cycle</li><li>SFPP Cycle</li><li>Pre-Vac Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory steam sterilizers operate through different cycles to ensure the effective eradication of microorganisms. The Gravity Cycle relies on gravity to allow steam penetration, making it suitable for liquids and solids. The SFPP (Steam Flush Pressure Pulse) Cycle uses a series of steam flushes and pressure pulses to enhance sterilization for devices with porous loads. The Pre-Vac Cycle employs a vacuum to remove air before steam penetration, ensuring thorough sterilization of complex instruments and hollow devices. Each cycle caters to specific sterilization needs.</p></p>
