<p><p>The Display Light Control Film market is divided into two main types based on substrate material: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) substrates and Non-PET substrates. PET substrates are widely used due to their durability, flexibility, and excellent optical properties, making them suitable for various electronic displays. Non-PET substrates include materials like glass or other polymers, offering unique advantages such as enhanced dimensional stability or specific light-filtering qualities. Both types cater to different applications in consumer electronics, automotive displays, and architectural lighting.</p></p>
