<p><p>The Direct Bonded Copper (DBC) substrate market comprises various types, notably the AlN (Aluminum Nitride) and Al2O3 (Alumina) DBC ceramic substrates. AlN DBC substrates are known for their excellent thermal conductivity and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for power electronics and LEDs. In contrast, Al2O3 DBC substrates offer good electrical insulation and chemical resistance at a lower cost, suitable for applications requiring moderate thermal performance. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing the efficiency of electronic devices.</p></p>
