<p><strong>The Signature Verification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Signature Verification</li><li>Dynamic Signature Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Signature verification encompasses two primary market types: static and dynamic. Static signature verification analyzes a handwritten signature's image for characteristics like shape and size, assessing authenticity through comparison with stored samples. In contrast, dynamic signature verification examines the behavioral patterns of the signing process, including speed, pressure, and rhythm, offering real-time authentication. Both methods serve to enhance security in various applications, such as banking and legal documents, addressing the need for reliable means to verify identity and prevent fraud.</p></p>
