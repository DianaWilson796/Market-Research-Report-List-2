<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>SMD/SMT</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncoupled dual inductor market is segmented into three main types: radial, SMD/SMT, and through-hole. Radial inductors feature leads that extend from the bottom, allowing for compact board designs. SMD/SMT inductors are designed for surface mounting technology, promoting higher assembly efficiency and allowing for smaller device footprints. Through-hole inductors have leads that pass through the circuit board, providing robust connections suited for high-reliability applications. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs and design considerations in electronic products.</p></p>
