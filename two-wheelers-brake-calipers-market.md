<p><p>The Two Wheeler Brake Calipers market is primarily divided into two types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to clamp the brake pads onto the rotor, providing adequate stopping power for smaller bikes and scooters. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons, enhancing braking performance and heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-performance motorcycles. This differentiation allows manufacturers to cater to various segments, balancing cost and performance based on rider needs.</p></p>
