<p><p>The dermatoscopy market is divided into two main types: traditional dermatoscopes and digital dermatoscopes. In contrast, digital dermatoscopes integrate advanced imaging technology, allowing for high-resolution photos and video capture of skin conditions. This digital approach enhances diagnostic accuracy, facilitates remote consultations, and supports better documentation and analysis of skin-related issues, catering to evolving healthcare needs.</p></p>
