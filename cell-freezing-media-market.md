<p><p>The cell freezing media market is divided into two main types: Fetal Bovine Serum (FBS) media and no FBS media. FBS media incorporates serum, providing essential growth factors and nutrients that enhance cell viability during cryopreservation. This type is often preferred for its ability to improve recovery rates post-thawing. In contrast, no FBS media is designed for animal-free applications, making it ideal for ethical considerations and use in specialized research contexts, such as in stem cell and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
