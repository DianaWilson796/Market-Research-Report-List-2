<p><ul><li>Quick Disconnect Fuses</li><li>Slow-Breaking Fuses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-time fuse markets are categorized mainly into quick disconnect fuses and slow-breaking fuses. Quick disconnect fuses offer rapid response to overcurrent, making them suitable for applications requiring immediate protection, such as in electronics and automotive circuits. In contrast, slow-breaking fuses are designed to tolerate temporary overloads, providing delayed disconnection, which is beneficial in motor applications where inrush currents occur. Both types are essential for safeguarding electrical systems and ensuring reliable operation across various industries.</p></p>
