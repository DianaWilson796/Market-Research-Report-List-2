<p><p>Lab microplate readers are analytical instruments used to measure biological, chemical, or physical properties of samples in microplates. The market is divided into multi-mode and single-mode microplate readers. Multi-mode microplate readers offer versatility by providing various detection methods, such as absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence, enabling diverse applications. In contrast, single-mode microplate readers specialize in one detection method, such as absorbance or fluorescence, making them ideal for specific tasks where high precision is essential but lacking versatility.</p></p>
