<p><ul><li>Articulated</li><li>Rail-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Instrument Holding Arm market consists of two primary types: articulated and rail-mounted systems. Articulated arms feature multiple joints that allow for flexible positioning of instruments, offering precise adjustments for various procedures. In contrast, rail-mounted systems utilize tracks or rails to secure instruments, providing stable support with the ability to reposition along the length of the rail. Both types enhance ergonomic efficiency and facilitate better access during medical procedures, ultimately improving patient outcomes and reducing surgical fatigue.</p></p>
